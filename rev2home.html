Iconoclast Boards

Iconoclast custom surboards epitomizes a fusion of iconic design principles with innovative and sustainble materials.

Iconoclast

(I'kon'e-klast')

n., A surfer who defies convention, seeks own line-up.

Synonyms: Non-conformist, Individualist, Maverick

Antonyms: Conformist, Sheep

The Goal?

Produce equipment with not only a smaller eco-footprint but create beautiful aesthetics and improved functionality.
By incorporating displacement hull theories from legends like Bob Simmons and George Greenough up front, paired with planing hull concaves and channels in the rear, along with advanced natural materials like flax-basalt, iconoclast aims to achieve a more dynamic ride. Their ‘Eco Warrior’ line goes a step further, using Entropy bio-resin and experimenting with agave blanks from ECOBOARD “Gold Level” member Grow Blanks in Kenya.
